Section 05

3rd Party Integrations — Full Map

OpenTable operates as a platform hub connecting diners, restaurants, payment systems, travel platforms, marketing tools, and AI services. Every integration identified from live scraping of opentable.com and /restaurant-solutions/integrations/.

🔍
Integrations discovered via: Partner Benefits page (/user/profile/benefits), City landing pages (Chase Sapphire Reserve banner), Restaurant Solutions Integrations page (partner logo wall: Google, Booking.com, Aloha, TripAdvisor, Klaviyo, Yext, Slang, Meta, Uber Eats, Trip.com, Stripe, Apple, Wisely, Jobvite, Cventand more), and FAQ / account creation flows (Google/Facebook/Apple OAuth).

Diner-Facing (Consumer Integrations)

Authentication / SSO
Google OAuth
Sign up / Sign in with Google. Reduces friction on booking gate. Single-click account creation.
Facebook OAuth
Sign up / Sign in with Facebook. Populates profile name and email automatically.
Apple Sign In
Sign in with Apple ID on iOS devices. Privacy-first (hide-my-email option). Native iOS integration.
Partner Benefits / VIP Access
Uber One
Uber One subscribers get "Exclusive Tables" — primetime slots at popular restaurants not visible to standard users. Linked via Uber account connection in Partner Benefits. OpenTable VIPs also get free 6-month Uber One membership.
Chase Sapphire Reserve
Sapphire Reserve cardmembers get exclusive table access + $300 annual dining credit. Displayed as promo banner on city landing pages. Card linked via Chase portal redirect.
Visa Dining Collection
Visa cardholders access primetime tables and special events. Verified through Visa card link in Partner Benefits page.
Rewards Redemption
Amazon
OpenTable points can be redeemed for Amazon gift cards. Fulfillment handled via Amazon Gift Card API — email delivery.
In-venue credit
Bill credit redeemed directly at the restaurant on day of dining — handled through restaurant's POS integration.
Parent Company / Travel
Booking Holdings (Parent)
OpenTable is owned by Booking Holdings — shares infrastructure, data, and cross-platform loyalty with Booking.com, Priceline, Kayak, Agoda.
Booking.com
Hotel guests can discover and book OpenTable restaurants. Cross-platform recommendations visible in both apps.
Trip.com
OpenTable restaurant bookings surfaced within Trip.com travel itineraries. Visible in restaurant solutions partner wall.
TripAdvisor
OpenTable reservation widget embedded on TripAdvisor restaurant pages — cross-platform booking integration.
AI & Voice
Slang AI
AI voice agent for restaurants — answers phone inquiries, books reservations via voice, routes to OpenTable. Visible on restaurant integrations page and referenced in AI Concierge partnerships.
Microsoft Copilot
AI Concierge partnerships for extending OpenTable's natural language restaurant search into Microsoft's AI assistant ecosystem.
Amazon Alexa+
Voice-activated restaurant discovery and booking through Alexa+ smart home integration.
Discovery / SEO
Google Search / Maps
OpenTable reservation widgets appear directly in Google Search results ("Reserve a table" on restaurant Knowledge Panels). Critical discovery channel.
Zagat
Zagat ratings integrated into restaurant profiles — OpenTable acquired Zagat's brand/data to enrich editorial content and credibility signals.
Yext
Restaurant listing management and local search sync — ensures consistent info across Google, Apple Maps, Yelp, Bing.

Restaurant-Facing (B2B Integrations)

Point-of-Sale (POS)
Aloha by NCR
Bi-directional sync between OpenTable reservations and Aloha POS — guest spend data feeds back to guest profiles for CRM. Visible in partner logo wall.
Toast
Reservation and POS data sync — table turn times, check averages linked to guest profiles and analytics dashboards.
Square, Lightspeed & others
OpenTable's API supports multiple POS providers for payment and spend data integration.
Delivery Platforms
Uber Eats
OpenTable restaurant partners can integrate with Uber Eats for delivery alongside their dine-in reservation system. Referral fee model. Visible in restaurant solutions partner wall.
Olo
Online ordering and delivery middleware — bridges restaurant ordering systems with OpenTable's guest profiles.
DoorDash (via Olo)
Indirect delivery integration through Olo middleware connector.
Marketing & CRM
Klaviyo
Email marketing automation — guest data from OpenTable syncs to Klaviyo for segmented campaigns, re-engagement flows, and birthday/anniversary triggers.
Meta (Facebook/Instagram Ads)
Restaurant promotional ads and retargeting — guest data used for look-alike audience creation. Pixel integration for conversion tracking.
Wisely
Guest intelligence / CRM platform — enriches OpenTable guest profiles with spend data, visit frequency, and loyalty scoring for restaurant operators.
Yext (also B2B)
Restaurant-side: manages business listings, menus, and hours across directories from within OpenTable dashboard.
Payments
Stripe
Payment processing for credit card guarantees on reservations, prepaid dining experiences, and no-show fees. Visible in restaurant solutions partner wall.
Apple Pay
Native iOS payment for in-app bookings requiring payment hold. Available through Apple integration.
Hospitality / Events
Cvent
Event management platform for private dining and large group bookings — connects event RFPs to OpenTable's private dining module.
Tripleseat
Private event and banquet management integration for restaurant groups managing large-party reservations.
APIs / Developer
OpenTable API (Public)
REST API for 3rd party developers to embed reservation widgets, access availability data, and build custom booking flows. Documented at /restaurant-solutions/resources.
Webhook events
Reservation created / modified / cancelled events pushed to restaurant's systems in real-time.
reCAPTCHA (Google)
Bot protection on restaurant onboarding forms and diner sign-up. Applied to Marketo form submissions.
